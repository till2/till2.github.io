### Why you should read & how to read more

Well, let me start by saying that books just smell good. Jokes aside, I recommend you watch <a href="https://youtu.be/lIW5jBrrsS0" target="_blank"> BOOKSTORES: How to Read More Books in the Golden Age of Content</a> before reading this post. It motivates why you and I should read more books and how to do it. I really do hope that you give this video a shot, but be aware that it might really get you excited to find some good books to read, and then there is no stopping. You'll be hooked aswell. Also, consider all the benefits of reading:

- knowledge
- sparking of ideas
- forced meditation (increases attention span & trains focus) and reflection periods
- immersion & fun
- comfy activity (either read outside, in a cafe, in a library or in the evening with some nice ambience)
- low dopamine (reduces the baseline to make other productive activities more fun)

... so i would turn the question around and ask why shouldn't you read! (If you come up with something, feel free to write a [comment](https://till2.github.io/comments/).)
